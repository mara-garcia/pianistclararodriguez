Collaboration with soloists and orchestras

Some of the artists and orchestras she has performed in concerts with include clarinettist and conductor Michael Collins, violinist concertmaster of the BBC Symphony Orchestra, Stephen Bryant, guitarists Alirio Díaz and Miguel Delgado-Estévez, El Cuarteto ensemble, members of the Orchestra of the Age of Enlightenment, Orquesta Sinfónica Simón Bolívar, Sinfónica de Venezuela, Orquesta Filarmónica Nacional de Venezuela, Orquesta Sinfónica de Maracaibo, Orquesta Sinfónica Francisco de Miranda, Orquesta Sinfónica Juan José Landaeta,  Phoenix Orchestra, Orquesta de Aragua, conductors Jordi Mora, Marc Dooley, Levon Parikian, Christopher Adey, Eduardo Rahn, Teresa Hernández, Carlos Riazuelo, Alfredo Rugeles, Rodolfo Saglimbeni, Luis Miguel González-Fuentes, Telésforo Naranjo, Jesús Uzcátegui, violinists Claudio González, Freya Goldmark, cellists Corentin Chassard and Jordan Gregoris, percussionists Carlos Nené Quintero, Orlando Poleo, Wilmer Sifontes, Geniver Graci and Manuel Rangel, accordionist Federico Ruiz, bassists Leonardo Muller-Rodriguez, Gabriel León and Carlos Rodriguez, cuatristas Raúl Delgado-Estévez, Edwin Arellano and Eduardo Ramirez, mandolinist Cristóbal Soto, flautist Efra Oscher, José Antonio Naranjo, trombonist Adrián Suárez, pianist Guiomar Narváez.
