Pedagogy

As a staff member of the Royal College of Music Junior Department since 2006, her students have played at the Wigmore Hall, the Albert Hall (Elgar Room) and have been successful in the Royal Philharmonic Society Duet Competition.

Her students have entered all the major conservatoires and universities of the UK and have won scholarships to the Guildhall School of Music, The Royal Northern College of Music and Trinity College.

With an experience of over 25 years, Clara Rodriguez has been an active and highly sought-after private teacher of students who have travelled from as far afield as India, France, different corners of the UK and Venezuela to receive tuition from her.

Her students have won many competitions and scholarships and are pursuing successful music careers all over the world. She is much in demand as an adjudicator, gives international masterclasses as well as online tuition.
