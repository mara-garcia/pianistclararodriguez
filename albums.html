GERSHWIN ET AL

Americas Without Frontiers
Rodriguez (piano)
Nimbus Alliance NI6346

"The Venezuelan is a doughty champion of Latin-American music, and her programme of piano pieces reaches below and often jaunty surface to capture the region's deeper soul. Plenty is captivating, not least Venezuelan works by Antonio Estévez and Juan Carlos Núñez, while Gershwin's Three Preludes are a class act".

The Sunday Times

These albums have featured in programmes such as CD Review on BBC Radio 3 and have been reviewed worldwide by all the major classical music magazines and newspapers including The Sunday Times Culture Magazine, On Record -The latest essential releases–

They are constantly played on BBC Radio 3 and on networks around the world.

"The music might be undemanding to hear but it is certainly not undemanding to play; and, as with her previous engaging disc of her compatriot Teresa Carreño, Clara Rodriguez makes the most of her innate feel for the exuberant and languorous, dispatching the toe-tapping cross-rhythms with panache and a light touch. Almost any of these, but particularly Paesano’s Pajarillo, Bor’s Fuga and Fernández El diablo suelto, would make a refreshing encore. This is a treasure chest from which to cherry-pick".

Jeremy Nicholas. Gramophone Magazine
